    What is Information Technology?

    An information technology (IT) major is a college degree program that focuses on the use of computers and technology to solve problems and manage information. IT majors typically study computer science, business, and communications. Along the way, they may focus on one specialty such as web development or digital communications. But regardless of their focus, they will acquire strong technical and communication skills.
